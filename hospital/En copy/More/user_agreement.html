Welcome to Sultan Bin Abdulaziz Humanitarian City, the oasis of hope. We are thrilled to have you here in a virtual tour to give you an idea about the city and its mission, and provide you with information about all the services that you might need. The City’s website shows our commitment to our patients and to our community, which is reflected by our mission, “helping people to help themselves.” Since it was founded by the late crown price, HRH Prince Sultan Bin Abdulaziz in 2002, the City has treated more than 600,000 patients from all over the Middle East. Most of them were able recover and went back to their normal life and reengaged into their community. In the City, we don’t believe that “Disability” is a barrier. Instead, we believe in motivation and hope which are the theme behind all the great success stories that our patients lived during the last ten years. Our organizational culture is characterized by our passion for excellence philosophy. Such passion reflects our starving to be the best in all what we do, and provide the highest quality of care as we can, based on the Patient Centered Care concept. The City is honored to be accredited and certified by the top international accreditation organizations in the world such as The Joint Commission (JCI) and The Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities (CARF). We are also honored for the high satisfaction rate by our dear patients and the awards that we achieved during our years of operation such as: the award of best healing environment, the award of excellence in patient safety, the award of excellence in patient centered care, and our award of excellence in hospital education and training. In the City we are confident with the services and the quality of care that we provide. We are also proud of all of our staff, specialist, and physicians who are behind all of our achievements. With them we assure that you are in good hands and your experience with us will be a memorable one. Thank you and God bless you.
