WHAT IT TAKES

If you are a hardworking, forward looking individual or team with a solution-driven idea then we welcome you to take part in our program.

TO INSPIRE IS TO EMPOWER

The Kingdom's First corporate Incubator/Accelerator for startups addressing ICT/Digital innovation

How it Works

Selection

  • Based on pre-determined selection criteria, the Top 30 applications will proceed to the next stage
  • Selected teams will go through a one-on-one interview and will be required to submit a 1 minute movie allowing us to know them better
  • An internal review, of the interviews and movies will shorten the list of applicants from 30 to 10. The Top 10 teams will be invited to our two-day bootcamp event

Application

Start by filling the application form available on this website.

Consider your application as a key step to share with us your idea/product in a structured approach and gain our initial interest.

Program start

Right after the bootcamp, InspireU will initiate the onboarding activities with the winners to help them relocate, sign the agreement and be ready to embark in a 6 months program.

Bootcamp

This two-day event will help you structure your idea from both a business and technical perspective through a set of workshops.

The first day workshop, conducted by highly skilled mentors, has for purpose to add value to your idea and prepare you for the final pitch.

The second day will focus on all teams pitching their ideas in front of 5 judges and answering questions and inquiries.

Winners will be announced after the pitching.
