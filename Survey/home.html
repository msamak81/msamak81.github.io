employees’ satisfaction survey

This survey aims to measure employees’ satisfaction within eChannels, and also to understand the challenges employees’ face, in order to improve employee satisfaction. Your feedback is valuable so make sure to answer the questions with total honesty. This survey takes about 20 minutes to complete, and the outcome aims to benefit you as an employee. We care greatly about your opinion and your answers will be taken seriously. Note that your individual answers will remain anonymous and results will be reported in combination with all participants. Individual answers will not be shared with any colleague, manager, or department inside or outside the company. Keep in mind that your name is not required in the survey.
