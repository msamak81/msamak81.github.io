Features

Register Fingerprint

  • Register existing postpaid SIM card with finger print.
  • Register existing prepaid SIM card with finger print.
  • Register existing prepaid Quicknet SIM card with finger print.

New SIM Cards

  • New postpaid SIM cards with finger print registration.
  • New SAWA SIM cards with finger print registration.
  • New SAWA Zeyara SIM cards with finger print registration.

Ownership Transfer

  • Transfer SAWA ownership to existing STC customer.
  • Transfer SAWA ownership to new customer
  • Transfer postpaid Quicknet ownership to existing STC customer
  • Transfer postpaid Quicknet ownership to new customer
