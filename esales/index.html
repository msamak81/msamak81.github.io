STC eSales

STC eSales app is one of STC Sales channels to sell Prepaid SIM Cards, and Register Prepaid Customers fingerprint.
* This app should be used only by STC agents not by STC customers.

App Features

  • New SAWA Prepaid with Finger Print
  • New Quicknet Prepaid with Finger Print
  • New SAWA Zeyara with Finger Print
  • Register current Postpaid SIM card with Finger print
  • Register current SAWA SIM card with Finger Print
  • Register Current Postpaid internet package with Finger Print
  • Transfer SAWA Ownership to current Customer
  • Transfer SAWA Ownership to New Customer
  • Transfer prepaid Quicknet Ownership to current Customer
  • Transfer Prepaid Quicknet Ownership to New Customer
