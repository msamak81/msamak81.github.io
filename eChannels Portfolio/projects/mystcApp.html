MySTC APP

Release Date 12/11/2016
Current Version 3.4v
Supported Platform

DESCRIPTION

MySTC 3 MOBILE APPLICATIONMySTC App gives you the ability to control and deal with your own mobile and landline numbers in STC by providing you a lot of services and features that help you to do your operations easily, like viewing the bills and pay them, recharge prepaid numbers and transfer amounts between them, subscribe and unsubscribe in packages and services, monitor your data usage, view qitaf points and redeem them with gifts, locate branches and wifi spots in the kingdom, and a lot more features that saves your time and effort

FEATURES

  • Bills Management

    View your bills details and pay them using credit card, also you can pay a single bill or multiple bills.

  • Data Usage

    Monitor your active data package to see your usage and how many days left for it to expire.

  • Qitaf Redemption

    View the number of Qitaf points you gained and redeem it with different redemption options like Qitaf rewards Alfursan miles, donations and much more.

  • Locations

    Locate the nearest STC branch and see the services provided in this branch, also locate STC WiFi spots and check the network coverage on a specific spot.

  • Balance Transfer

    Transfer a balance from prepaid to prepaid number or postpaid to prepaid number and request a balance transfer from someone else.

  • Prepaid Recharge

    Recharge your prepaid mobile numbers using recharge cards or directly using credit card.

  • Subscription Management

    Find a package or a service that suits your needs and subscribe for it directly from the app, and also you can unsubscribe from it if not needed.

  • Support

    There are many different support options if needed like Live Support, Contact Form, How To Videos and much more.
